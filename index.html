Welcome to

Established in 1961, Crestview offers a scenic combination of doglegs, creeks, ponds and mature trees.

A new design has enhanced this challenging 18 hole natural setting. The front nine is 2,792 yards from the blue tees, and the back nine is 3,225 yards.

Off the course, the clubhouse offers a Pro Shop, snack bar, full banquet facilities, and always a friendly staff. Junior and senior rates are also available.
